Blackjack

Author: Mike Zhukovskiy

Overview

A blackjack game written in Sinatra for Tealeaf Academy's first course.
Uses jquery and the Twitter Bootstrap CSS library.

Navigate to '/' to play.

Rules

  • Try to get as close to 21 as possible without going over (busting).
  • Choose "Hit" to be dealt another card or "Stay" to see the dealer's cards
  • Dealer will hit as long as its total is less than or equal to 17
